# ⚕️ Panacea Sound
### The open-source cure for the subscription epidemic.

**Panacea Sound** is a zero-dependency, browser-native procedural music engine. It is designed for YouTubers, podcasters, and game developers who are tired of paying "protection money" to licensing giants like Epidemic Sound or Artlist.

Most "AI music" tools are black boxes that require an internet connection, a subscription, and a data-harvesting account. **Panacea is the parallel infrastructure.** It runs locally, offline, and generates high-fidelity, royalty-free audio using pure mathematics—not neural networks trained on stolen data.

---

## 🚀 Why Panacea? (The Pitch)

### 1. Stop the Subscription Leak
In 2026, the average "Creator Pro" license can cost upwards of **$200/year**. If you stop paying, your rights to future use often vanish. Panacea is **Free Forever**.

### 2. Human-First Procedural Logic (No AI)
Panacea doesn't "hallucinate" music. It uses a procedural composition engine that follows musical theory—scales, timing, and arrangement—defined by human code.

* **Authentic Control:** You aren't "prompting" a machine; you are configuring an architect.
* **Ethics-Clean:** No training sets. No copyright gray areas. No "AI-generated" flags from platforms.

### 3. Built for Your Workflow
* **Infinite Loops:** Perfect for streamers, background ambience, and game levels.
* **Full-Length Tracks:** Configurable "Song Mode" for podcasts, intros, and cinematic transitions.
* **Uncompressed Export:** Download your creations as **high-fidelity .WAV files** (not lossy MP3s) to ensure professional-grade production.

---

## 🛠 Technical Advantages

* **Zero Dependencies:** No external libraries, no React, no trackers. A single self-contained HTML/JS file.
* **Mobile Ready:** Runs in any mobile browser. Generate a full podcast intro on your phone while on the bus.
* **Offline-First:** Works in air-gapped environments. Your data never leaves your machine because there is no server to send it to.
* **Ultra-Lightweight:** The entire engine is smaller than a single high-res thumbnail image.

---

## 📂 How to Use

1.  **Download:** Grab the `index.html` file from this repo.
2.  **Launch:** Open it in Chrome, Firefox, or Safari (Desktop or Mobile).
3.  **Configure:** Select your "Vibe" (Lo-Fi, Jazz, Synth, etc.).
4.  **Generate:** Hit "Compose" to hear your unique track.
5.  **Export:** Click "Download WAV" and drop it straight into Premiere, DaVinci, or Unity.
